You can extract all of the variables in use from a Smarty template using a simple call to `*nix` grep with a trivial regex expression. Here is the command-

{% highlight bash %}
grep -o "\{\$[-_a-zA-Z0-9]*\}" my-template.tpl
{% endhighlight %}
